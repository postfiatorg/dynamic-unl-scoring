## Addendum — activation-fix verification (rounds 307–310, 2026-07-02)

An independent review of the hardening after the campaign found one regression
the campaign could not see (nothing crashes): the VL `effective` activation
timestamp was still computed at signing time, so the deferred publication
distributed already-effective lists that validators activate at their
independent poll times — re-opening the staggered-activation window the
M1.10.6 lookahead exists to close. Fixed on `main` in `100f2af` (activation
anchored to the publication deadline, plus a delayed-resume re-sign guard that
only ever runs before the IPFS pin), alongside sidecar liveness follow-ups in
`validator-scoring-sidecar` `7c191e0`. Both were deployed to devnet and testnet
before these rounds. The rows keep the campaign's denominator discipline: every
triggered round has a row, and the two service outages below were deliberate
scenario injections.
